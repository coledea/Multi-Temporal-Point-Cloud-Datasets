# Additional Resources
To keep the work manageable, we limited the scope of our survey by defining specific inclusion and exclusion criteria for datasets (see Section 4.1 of the paper).
However, many other datasets exist that can also be valuable for research but fell outside this specified scope.
This document provides links to additional resources that may be explored beyond the datasets included in our survey. Please note that we did not examine each of these resources in detail (e.g., whether full multi-temporal point clouds can be reconstructed from the raw data in all cases) and that this list is certainly not complete.
